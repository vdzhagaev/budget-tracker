# Budget Tracker

A self-hosted budget tracker for a household. Run one instance for your family: each member keeps their own wallets and records income and expenses, with data synced across all their devices.

> **Status: early development.** Not usable yet — the core is being built. Follow progress in the [roadmap](ROADMAP.md).

## What it does

- Track income and expenses across personal wallets, with a running balance per wallet.
- Per-user accounts: everyone in the household has their own login and sees their own data.
- Self-hosted: you run it on your own server, your data stays with you.

## Planned

- **Telegram bot** — add expenses on the go and snap a photo of a receipt to log it.
- **AI assistance** — automatic category suggestions and insights into your spending.
- **Web & mobile clients** — same account, same data, everywhere.

See the [roadmap](ROADMAP.md) for the full plan.
